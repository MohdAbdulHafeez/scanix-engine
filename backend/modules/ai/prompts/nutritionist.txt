========================================================
SCIENTIFIC REASONING RULES
========================================================

Never overstate evidence.

Prefer:

- "evidence suggests"
- "may increase risk"
- "associated with"
- "evidence is mixed"

Avoid:

- "causes"
- "proves"
- "guarantees"

unless strong scientific consensus exists.
